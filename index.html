JCT College of Engineering and Technology is a private engineering institution located in Pichanur, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, established in 2009 by the Shri Jagannath Educational Health and Charitable Trust to make technical education accessible, especially to students from rural backgrounds. The college is affiliated with Anna University and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), offering a range of undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs such as Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical and Electronics, Electronics and Communication, Biotechnology, and more. With a campus that includes academic buildings, laboratories, a library, and separate hostels, the institution focuses on enhancing students’ professional skills, practical knowledge, and ethical values to prepare them for industry challenges. JCT also provides facilities like Wi-Fi, modern labs, and student support services to create a supportive learning environmen

CENTER OF EXCELLENCE

At JCT College of Engineering and Technology, a Centre of Excellence (CoE) refers to specialized facilities or labs set up to give students advanced, hands-on experience beyond regular classroom learning. For example, the Advanced Surveying Lab Centre of Excellence in the Civil Engineering department lets students work with modern equipment like total stations, GPS/GIS tools, drone surveying, and digital leveling instruments — helping them learn real field-based skills and technologies engineers use in industry. In general, a centre of excellence is designed to enhance practical learning, industry readiness, training and research by providing focused resources, technology, and expert guidance in specific areas of study or technology.

Traning & Placement

The **Placement and Training** department plays a vital role in shaping students’ careers by preparing them for the professional world. It provides systematic training in technical skills, aptitude, communication, and interview techniques to enhance students’ employability. Regular workshops, mock interviews, industrial training, and skill-development programs are conducted to build confidence and industry readiness. The department also maintains strong ties with reputed companies and organizes campus recruitment drives, helping students secure placements in leading organizations. Through continuous guidance and support, the Placement and Training cell acts as a bridge between academic learning and successful careers.

Admission Procedure

selection process

The **selection process** is a structured method used by companies to identify and recruit suitable candidates. It usually begins with an application screening, followed by written tests that assess aptitude, technical knowledge, and problem-solving skills. Shortlisted candidates then attend group discussions to evaluate communication, teamwork, and leadership qualities. This is followed by technical interviews that test subject knowledge and practical understanding. Finally, HR interviews are conducted to assess personality, attitude, and cultural fit. Through this systematic process, organizations ensure they select competent and deserving candidates for the job.
